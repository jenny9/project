Television top-50 over the years A data visualisation

The purpose of this data visualisation is to give insight in the makeup of the television top 50 of the Netherlands. The top 50 consists of the 50 broadcasts with the most viewers.

Data processing

In the upper graph is shown per year which channels had how many contributions to the top 50. The user can choose to view the data of the channels separately (by selecting 'All') or to view the data of the channels grouped into public and commerical channels (by selecting 'Groups'). By clicking the colored rectangles on the legend of this graph, lines can be added and removed.



In the lower graph, the content top 50 of the selected year is shown. The year can be selected by either moving the cursor over the upper graph, or clicking one of the buttons left to the lower graph. By hovering over the circles in the lower graph, more information of that specific broadcast is shown. Placing the cursor on the graph and scrolling enables vertical zooming. The graph can also be dragged vertically. More insight in the makeup of this top 50 can be obtained by clicking one of the buttons 'Channel', 'Time' or 'Day'. When clicked, the circles will be colored according to their value on the respective button subject.
